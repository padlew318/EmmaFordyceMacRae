Head of a Child

There is no record of when MacRae painted Head of a Child. The child is looking directly at the viewer and the painting is similar in style to Portrait of Self painted in 1920.

Exhibited: 1930 – Roerich Museum, New York - Solo exhibition; 2008 – Cape Ann Museum
