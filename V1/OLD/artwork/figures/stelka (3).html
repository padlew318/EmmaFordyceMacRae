Emma Fordyce MacRae 20th Century American Artist

Emma Fordyce MacRae executed works that were noted for the complexity of their compositions, color, and careful technique, each dependent upon the other. She managed to integrate elements of the past and the present, the modern and the traditional, and yet at the same time she instilled her oils with an aura of agelessness. This complexity is uniquely her own and indeed makes it worth taking the time to linger over her paintings.
