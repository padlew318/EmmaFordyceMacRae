Dogwood with Madonna

Emma Fordyce MacRae

Sometimes, especially in her later work, she chose a plain background in a strong or neutral tone as seen in Dogwood with Madonna, the colors of the backgrounds either echoed the palette of the flowers or contrasted to them and MacRae usually featured a single type of flower. These settings drew positive responses for MacRae’s work; one reviewer noted “she has used figured backgrounds behind her flowers daringly but with pleasing effect”.
