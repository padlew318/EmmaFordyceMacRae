Help with PDF files

Adobe Acrobat Portable Document Format (PDF). Adobe Reader enables you to read PDFs. The latest versions of Adobe Reader (7.0+) offer more in the way of accessibility than earlier versions. You can find the latest version of Adobe Reader to download for free on the Adobe website - Opens in a new window.

You can download a useful document about how to access PDFs with various assistive technologies from the Adobe website - Opens in a new window.

You can also find tools and information to increase the accessibility of PDF documents on the Adobe website - Opens in a new window.
