Paintings by Emma Fordyce MacRae can be seen at The Cape Ann Museum in Gloucester or the Museum of Fine Art in Boston.

The Cape Ann Museum at 27 Pleasant Street has a Self Portrait as well as Pigeon Cove in its collection of items relating to the fishing industry. The catalogue for the Emma Fordyce MacRae exhibition held at the Museum is found in the library downstairs or on Cape Ann Museum, Gloucester, MA Catalogue.

Another day it may be fun to take the train into Boston to visit the Museum of Fine Arts. There are two figure paintings by Emma Fordyce MacRae in their collection but not on display. The paintings are called "Beatrice" and "Ruth Goldfarb With Green Fascinator". You would have to make a special request to view them, more information about these paintings can be found on the mfa.org website.
